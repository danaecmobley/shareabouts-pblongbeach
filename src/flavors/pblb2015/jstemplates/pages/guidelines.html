{{#_}}Guidelines{{/_}}

  • {{#_}}Anyone can post ideas.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Eligible ideas must be for "capital" projects: lasting physical infrastructure for public benefit, like park improvements or new windows for school classrooms. "Expense" projects, like afterschool programs or expanding bus service, are not eligible.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}To add an idea, drag the map until the crosshairs are over the desired location. Only ideas within the boundaries of Council District 9 will be considered.{{/_}}
